About me

I am currently a master’s student at Shanghai Jiao Tong University, advised by Prof. Guangtao Xue. Before SJTU, I received my B.Eng. in Computer Science in 2022 from Hangzhou Dianzi University.

My CV provides more details about my experiences.

Research Interest

I currently focus on database systems, distributed systems, and storage systems. My previous research also includes deep learning. Therefore, I also look forward to integrating DL with various systems and designing systems for deep learning tasks, specifically ML4Sys and Sys4ML.

As I delve deeper into DL research during my undergraduate study, I increasingly realize that big data plays a significant role in society. I became more interested in building a system to manage intensive data. Nevertheless, we cannot deny that DL influences aspects of our lives, including systems. I believe that using ML as a decision-maker can significantly improve the efficiency of systems, and new systems are necessary to train and deploy them more efficiently.

Experience

Deep Learning Research

System Projects

I have done some open courses for distributed systems and database systems.

  • TinyKV is a course published by PingCAP. This is a simplification of their industrial product TiKV based on the design of Google Spanner.
  • BusTub is an educational database management system published by CMU 15-445 teaching group. It requires us to implement the system from the bottom of the database to the top layer.
  • MIT 6.824 provided an opportunity for me to implement a multi-thread Raft algorithm. The sharded KV engine is different from the design of TinyKV.
